Website Design

Web design comprises of numerous visual elements such as layout, color, graphics, fonts and content. Anybody can create a website, what's difficult is creating a user friendly design. All websites I create are mobile first device responsive applications. This means websites are easy to navigate, interactive and compatible across all devices and browsers.

Web Development

Where as web design can be completed relatively quickly using content management systems (CMSs) such as Wordpress, Magento and Drupal web development involves the use of coding to perform advanced interactivity and visual effects. I hold a number of web development certifications uncluding the following: PHP, MySQL, Javascript, Angular, Cortana.

Wordpress CMS training

When using content management systems such as Wordpress it is possible for non technical staff to perform basic updates after minimal training. Having staff trained to update your website means the site can be kept up to date at minimal expense. Also this is a big plus in terms of security - knowing that only your own employees have accessed the site.

Website registration

After purchasing an address you must pay a web hsoting company to host your website on their servers. I offer all inclusive website design packages so you don't have to worry about any of the extraneous details.

Website maintenance

In terms of SEO - getting your website high on google search results websites need to be updated regularly. I offer a year long maintenance package as standard to keep your website secure.
